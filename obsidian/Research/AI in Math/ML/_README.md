## Research/AI in Math/ML/

**What belongs here:** Paper summaries where the primary methodological contribution is **ML applied to mathematics in a non-RL, non-fully-agentic way** — specifically:

- Equation / conjecture discovery (symbolic regression, ML-generated mathematical conjectures)
- Neural heuristic guidance inside existing solvers (premise selection, clause selection, algorithm selection)
- Embeddings for math (dense representations of theorems, lemmas, mathematical objects)
- ML-guided algorithm / program search (evolutionary + LLM search over function/program spaces)

**Does not belong here:** Fully agentic proof systems (→ `Research/AI in Math/Agents/`), RL-trained provers or reward models (→ `Research/AI in Math/RL/`).

**Note**: LeanDojo/ReProver lives here (embedding + retrieval methodology) even though its output is proofs; FunSearch and AlphaEvolve live here (evolutionary ML search) even though both are sometimes called "agents" — the methodology is evolutionary/ML, not RL.

**Filename convention:** arXiv ID where available; `<first-author>-<year>-<slug>.md` for Nature-only papers.
