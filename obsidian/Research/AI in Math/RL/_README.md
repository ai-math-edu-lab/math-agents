## Research/AI in Math/RL/

**What belongs here:** Paper summaries where the primary methodological contribution involves **reinforcement learning applied to mathematical reasoning** — specifically:

- RL-guided tactic selection and proof search (value networks, policy networks, MCTS in mathematical settings)
- Process reward models (PRMs) for multi-step mathematical reasoning
- RL fine-tuning of LLMs specifically on mathematical tasks (GRPO, PPO variants for math)
- RL for mathematical algorithm discovery

**Does not belong here:** LLM-based proof search without an explicit RL component (→ `Research/AI in Math/Agents/`), pure ML/embedding approaches without RL (→ `Research/AI in Math/ML/`).

**Note**: AlphaProof and DeepSeek-Prover-V2 (RL-trained formal provers) live in `Research/AI in Math/Agents/` as their primary home (they are also agentic systems); the B3 synthesis cross-references them without duplicating their notes.

**Filename convention:** arXiv ID where available (`1805.07563.md`).
