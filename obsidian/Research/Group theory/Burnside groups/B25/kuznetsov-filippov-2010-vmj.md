# About the centralizer of an automorphism of order 2 of Burnside group B₀(2,5)

> **Translation note**: source paper is in Russian (`language: ru`). Abstract below is translated to English [trans.] from the mathnet.ru record.

## Abstract

The research examines the structure of the centralizer of an automorphism of order 2 of a special kind of Burnside group $B_0(2,5)$. The authors compute the generators, order, nilpotency class, and derived length, while constructing both the lower and upper central series. [trans.]

## TL;DR

Companion publication to [[kuznetsov-filippov-2010-sjim]]: same result (centralizer of an order-2 automorphism of $B_0(2,5)$, with generators, order, nilpotency class, derived length, lower and upper central series), shorter format (5 vs. 8 pages), different journal. Read [[kuznetsov-filippov-2010-sjim]] for the fuller treatment.

## Problem

Same as [[kuznetsov-filippov-2010-sjim]]: structure of $C_{B_0(2,5)}(\varphi)$ for an order-2 automorphism $\varphi$.

## Approach

Same as [[kuznetsov-filippov-2010-sjim]]. This shorter paper adds explicit construction of lower and upper central series (which may be in the longer paper too but is specifically highlighted here).

## Key result

Same as [[kuznetsov-filippov-2010-sjim]]:
- Generators of $C_{B_0(2,5)}(\varphi)$ found.
- Order, nilpotency class, derived length computed.
- Lower central series and upper central series explicitly constructed.
